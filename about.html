Serving the People of Gampaha Since 1866

District General Hospital Gampaha is committed to providing high-quality healthcare services to the community. With a team of experienced medical professionals and modern facilities, we ensure every patient receives compassionate and effective care.

Our Mission

Deliver accessible, high-quality healthcare to all citizens of Gampaha district, ensuring safety, dignity, and well-being.

Our Vision

To be a leading public hospital in Sri Lanka recognized for excellence in medical care, patient satisfaction, and community health.

Our Promise
